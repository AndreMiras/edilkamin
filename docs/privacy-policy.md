# Privacy Policy for Open Edilkamin

**Last updated**: December 25, 2025

## Data Collection

- **Edilkamin credentials**: Email and password sent directly to Edilkamin's servers for authentication
- **Authentication token**: Stored locally on your device
- **Fireplace MAC addresses**: Stored locally on your device
- **Bluetooth scanning**: Processed locally to discover nearby fireplaces, not transmitted externally

## Data Usage

Your data is used only to authenticate with Edilkamin and control your fireplace.

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. The App does not maintain any cloud servers or databases.

## Data Sharing

- **Edilkamin**: Credentials and commands are sent to Edilkamin's servers to operate your fireplace
- **No third parties**: No analytics, advertising, or data sharing with anyone else

## Security

All communication uses HTTPS encryption. The App is open-source: [github.com/AndreMiras/edilkamin](https://github.com/AndreMiras/edilkamin)

## Contact

Questions? Open an issue: [github.com/AndreMiras/edilkamin/issues](https://github.com/AndreMiras/edilkamin/issues)
